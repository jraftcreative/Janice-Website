My Journey

With over 15 years in Singapore's financial services industry, I've helped hundreds of families and individuals navigate their financial futures with confidence. My journey began in banking, where I quickly realized that most people needed personalized guidance — not generic products.

Today, I run an independent practice focused on one thing: helping you make informed financial decisions. Whether you're planning for retirement, protecting your family, or growing your investments, I bring clarity to complexity.
